Perry Mason

Erle Stanley Garder created the fictious lawyer after retiring from a career in Law in California. His writings were edited and became a radio program from 1943 to 1955. The TV series which began in 1957, featured the Canadian actor Raymond Burr. Burr initially auditioned for the part of Hamilton Burger, but Gardner declared that he was the embodiment of Perry Mason and it was a done deal! Earle Stanlry Gardner made an uncredited appearance as a Judge in the very last episode of the TV series.

The character Perry Mason was inspired by Earl Rogers, a trial attorney who appeared in 77 murder trials but lost only 3..
